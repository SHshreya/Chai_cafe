ABOUT US

Welcome to Chai Café — where every sip tells a story! We're not just a café; we're a cozy corner for chai lovers and creative thinkers. Whether you're here for the aroma, the flavor, or just the vibe, our doors are always open with a warm cup of chai.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is simple: bring people together over a cup of chai. We believe that tea is more than just a drink — it’s a culture, a conversation starter, and a moment of peace in a busy day. With a menu that blends traditional Indian flavors with a modern twist, we serve happiness in every cup.

HOW WE STARTED

Chai cafe began as a college side project — a fusion of two passions: building beautiful web experiences and enjoying endless cups of chai. What started as a small Django experiment has grown into a full-stack chai café app where users can browse, review, and order their favorite teas.
